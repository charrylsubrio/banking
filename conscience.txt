1. Invincibly Erroneous Conscience- mistakes cannot be avoided
2. Vincibly Erroneous Conscience- mistakes can be avoided with ordinary diligence
3. Perplexed Conscience- when a person has 2 options but fears that sin is present in both choices that either way feels he is doomed, the person must postpone any action in making a choice.
4. Pharisaical conscience- person magnifies small offenses as grave and serious.
5. Certain- judgment is sure that the action is morally good or evil.
6. Doubtful- the person hesitates in making a particular judgment.
7. Scrupulous- a very cautious or extremely fearful to make a decision or choice.
8. Lax- one that takes sinful acts very lightly and considers them as good and okay.
